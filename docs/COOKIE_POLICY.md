## üç™ What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

### Types of Storage Technologies

We use several technologies to store information:

- **Cookies**: Small files stored by your browser
- **Local Storage**: HTML5 web storage for larger data
- **Session Storage**: Temporary storage cleared when you close your browser
- **IndexedDB**: Database storage for offline functionality

When you first visit MyCRM, you'll see a cookie banner with options:
- **Accept All**: Consent to all cookies
- **Reject Non-Essential**: Only necessary cookies

**In the Application:**
1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Privacy**
2. Click **Cookie Preferences**

1. Preferences ‚Üí Privacy
2. Check "Block all cookies"

**Edge:**
1. Settings ‚Üí Cookies and site permissions ‚Üí Cookies and site data
2. Toggle "Block all cookies"

**‚ö†Ô∏è Warning:** Blocking all cookies will prevent you from logging into MyCRM.

**Delete Existing Cookies:**

- Chrome: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear browsing data ‚Üí Cookies
- Firefox: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear Data ‚Üí Cookies
- Safari: Preferences ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Manage Website Data ‚Üí Remove All

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Why do you need cookies?

**A:** Cookies enable core functionality (login, security) and help us improve the platform based on how it's used.

### Q: Can I use MyCRM without cookies?

**A:** You need strictly necessary cookies to log in and use the platform. Functional, analytics, and marketing cookies are optional.

### Q: Do cookies contain personal information?

**A:** Cookies contain identifiers (like session IDs), but not directly identifiable personal information like your name or email. However, they can be linked to your account.

### Q: Are cookies safe?

**A:** Yes. Cookies cannot:
- Install malware or viruses
- Access other files on your computer
- Carry viruses
- Read other cookies from different websites

### Q: How long do cookies last?

**A:** It depends:
- **Session cookies**: Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies**: Last from 24 hours to 2 years (see tables above)

### Q: Can I delete specific cookies?

**A:** Yes, in your browser's developer tools (F12) ‚Üí Application/Storage ‚Üí Cookies. However, it's easier to use our Cookie Preferences interface.

### Q: Will disabling cookies affect features?

**A:**
- ‚úÖ Strictly necessary: Cannot log in
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Functional: May lose preferences and customizations
- ‚ÑπÔ∏è Analytics: No impact on functionality
- ‚ÑπÔ∏è Marketing: No impact on functionality

### Q: Do you use cookies for email tracking?

**A:** No. Email tracking uses pixel beacons in emails, not cookies. This is separate from website cookies.
