## ğŸ”„ Plan Changes

### Upgrading

**Effective immediately:**
- Access new features right away
- Prorated charge for remainder of billing period
- New rate applies next billing cycle

**Example:**
- Current: Starter ($29/month), 15 days remaining
- Upgrade to: Professional ($79/month)
- Immediate charge: $25 (prorated $50 for 15 days)
- Next bill: $79 (full month)

### Downgrading

**Effective next billing cycle:**
- Keep current features until renewal
- Downgraded features listed in confirmation
- Export data before downgrade (if needed)

**Limitations:**
- May lose access to certain features
- Data over new plan limits becomes read-only
- Must reduce users to plan limit

### Cancellation

**Cancel anytime:**
- No cancellation fees
- Access continues until end of billing period
- Data retained for 90 days
- After 90 days: permanent deletion

**Refunds:**
- Monthly: Prorated within 7 days of charge
- Annual: Prorated within 30 days of initial purchase
- No refunds after 30 days (annual) or 7 days (monthly)

## â“ Pricing FAQ

### Q: Can I change plans anytime?

**A:** Yes! Upgrade immediately or downgrade at next renewal. No penalties.

### Q: What happens if I exceed my limits?

**A:** 
- **Users**: Must upgrade or remove users
- **Records**: Oldest records become read-only
- **Storage**: Upload blocked until upgraded
- **API**: Rate-limited (429 errors)

We notify you before limits are reached.

### Q: Do I pay for inactive users?

**A:** No. Deactivate users anytime. They don't count toward your user limit.

### Q: Can I get a discount for paying upfront?

**A:** Yes! Annual billing saves 20-25%. Multi-year Enterprise contracts save more.

### Q: Are there setup fees?

**A:** No setup fees for any plan.

### Q: Can I get a refund?

**A:** 
- Monthly: Prorated refund within 7 days
- Annual: Prorated refund within 30 days
- After grace period: No refunds, but can cancel anytime

### Q: Do you offer month-to-month for Enterprise?

**A:** Enterprise is annual only. Contact sales for exceptions.

### Q: Can I mix and match features?

**A:** Not on standard plans. Enterprise customers can customize.

### Q: What if I need more than 100K API calls/day?

**A:** Contact us for custom pricing. We can accommodate any volume.

### Q: Do you charge for integrations?

**A:** Standard integrations (Gmail, Slack, Zapier) are included. Premium integrations (Salesforce, SAP) cost $20-50/month each.
