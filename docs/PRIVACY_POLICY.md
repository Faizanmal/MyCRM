## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

**Encryption:**
- TLS 1.3 for data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Encrypted backups

**Access Controls:**
- Multi-factor authentication (2FA/MFA)
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Least-privilege principle
- Regular access reviews

**Infrastructure Security:**
- SOC 2 Type II certified data centers
- Firewalls and network segmentation
- DDoS protection
- Intrusion detection and prevention

**Application Security:**
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Vulnerability scanning
- Secure coding practices
- Input validation and output encoding

**Monitoring:**
- 24/7 security monitoring
- Automated threat detection
- Incident response procedures
- Regular security training for employees

### 4.2 Your Responsibilities

**Help us protect your account:**
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Don't share login credentials
- Log out from shared devices
- Report suspicious activity immediately

### 4.3 Data Breach Notification

If a breach occurs affecting your data:
- We will notify you within 72 hours
- We will describe the incident and impact
- We will explain our response and your options
- We will notify regulators as required by law

### 4.4 Limitations

**No system is 100% secure.** While we implement robust security measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You accept the inherent risks of internet transmission.

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Active Accounts

We retain your data while your account is active and as needed to provide the Service.

### 5.2 Account Deletion

When you delete your account:
- **Immediate**: Account deactivated, data inaccessible
- **90 days**: Data retained for recovery (if you change your mind)
- **After 90 days**: Data permanently deleted from production systems
- **Backups**: Purged from backups within 180 days

### 5.3 Legal and Business Requirements

We may retain certain data longer if required for:
- Legal compliance (e.g., tax records: 7 years)
- Dispute resolution
- Fraud prevention
- Enforcing our Terms

Such data is retained in secure, isolated storage.

### 5.4 Anonymized Data

Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for analytics and research.
